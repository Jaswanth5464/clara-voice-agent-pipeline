
Demo Call Transcript — Apex Fire Protection

Sales Rep: Hi! Thanks for joining the demo today. Can you tell me a bit about your business?

Client: Sure, we are Apex Fire Protection based in Newark New Jersey. We handle fire suppression, sprinkler systems and alarm monitoring for commercial buildings.

Sales Rep: What are your biggest pain points with call handling right now?

Client: Honestly the after hours calls are killing us. We get emergency calls at 2am, sprinkler leaks, fire alarms going off, and our receptionist is not available. Calls go to voicemail and customers panic.

Sales Rep: How do your emergency calls work currently?

Client: We have an on-call technician but customers don't always reach them. We need something that can identify if it is an emergency and connect them immediately.

Sales Rep: What about non-emergency calls?

Client: Those we can handle next day. Things like scheduling inspections, asking about pricing, general questions. After hours those can just leave a message.

Sales Rep: Do you use any service management software?

Client: Yes we use ServiceTrade for managing our jobs and dispatch.
