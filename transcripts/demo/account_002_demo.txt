Demo Call Transcript — BlueLine Sprinklers

Sales Rep: Hi! Tell me about your business.

Client: We are BlueLine Sprinklers based in Chicago. We install and maintain sprinkler systems for commercial buildings.

Sales Rep: What are your main pain points?

Client: We get a lot of after hours calls about sprinkler issues. Sometimes pipes burst at night and nobody answers.

Sales Rep: What counts as an emergency for you?

Client: Burst pipes, sprinkler head failures, water pressure loss in the system.

Sales Rep: Do you use any software?

Client: Yes we use ServiceTrade for all our jobs.

Sales Rep: What about non emergency calls?

Client: Inspection scheduling, maintenance requests, those can wait till next day.
